# Distilled World

## Premise
- Morrowsoil is a dead-adapted Earth-like world where zombie civilization inherited the surface after human collapse.
- Hidden origin involves a sky-fallen object and Morrow Seed altering death/memory/decay boundaries.

## Faction contrast
- **Surface settlements:** practical, fragile, domestic, worth protecting.
- **Pale Quarter (humans):** restorationist underground doctrine aiming to "correct" death via Redemption.

## Central threat
- **Dawn Bell** is the machine-scale extinction mechanism targeting adapted undead civilization.

## Tone
- Eerie but warm; lived-in decay over nihilistic horror.
