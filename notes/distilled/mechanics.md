# Distilled Mechanics

## Core verb family
- **Stillness:** inert undead state for stealth, sensor bypass, aggro manipulation.
- **Scent:** lure/control behavior + trigger specific environmental systems.
- **Kindly/Recoil:** shotgun as force mechanic for combat, traversal, and puzzle logic.
- **Nib:** scout, peck interaction, clue marking, omen signaling.

## Combat shape
- Exploration transitions into room-based tactical turns.
- Baseline player turn: move phase + 2 AP.
- Readability-first combat with low unit counts and authored objectives.

## Puzzle rules
- Puzzle identity must be protagonist-specific (small undead girl + bird + recoil).
- Mandatory rooms should combine at least two core verbs.
- Avoid generic key/door-only structure.
