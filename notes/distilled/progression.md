# Distilled Progression

## Campaign shape
- Prologue + 5 chapters + final chapter.
- Total target length: roughly 5–7 hours.

## Growth lanes
- **Kindly upgrades:** small set of story-gated utility/combat upgrades.
- **Nib upgrades:** scout/interaction range and utility improvements.
- **Lottie traits:** Stillness/scent/environmental tolerance enhancements.
- **Narrative flags:** dialogue + chapter gating are first-class progression.

## Economy/survival constraints
- Light survival pressure only (no heavy management sim).
- Small inventory with constrained item classes.
- Fast checkpoint resets; avoid long punishment loops.
